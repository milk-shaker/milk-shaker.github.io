OUR STORY

Our team comes with the experience and knowledge

We are a diverse team of five female Columbia engineers united by our personal connections to women who have faced mastitis and other breastfeeding challenges. Leveraging our combined expertise in mechanical and electrical engineering, prototyping, and research, along with leadership experience from campus organizations such as the Society of Women Engineers, American Society of Mechanical Engineers, Columbia’s Plasma Physics Laboratory, Columbia Space Initiative, and Columbia Formula Racing, we have developed MilkShaker to provide accessible, effective mastitis prevention.

Driven by friendship, shared experiences, and a deep commitment to women's health, we collaborate seamlessly to continually refine our innovative product. Our development process has been supported by invaluable partnerships with gynecologists, fellow engineers, women's health researchers, FemTech experts, and most notably, certified physical therapists from FemFirst Health. Their expert insights ensure that MilkShaker effectively and safely meets the real-world needs of breastfeeding women.

Breastfeeding is widely regarded as important to newborn development and mothers. Despite centuries of this practice, research and innovation in supporting new mothers has demonstrated yet another gaping hole in women’s health.

One specific and alarming problem faced by at least 1 in 10 breastfeeding mothers is mastitis, a condition that occurs when a clogged milk duct – affecting 1 in 5 – becomes infected, causing intense pain and stress for the mother and restricting milk production for the child. This occurs due to poor lymphatic drainage from postpartum posture changes.

Many mothers resort to at-home remedies or neglect the issue, enduring high fevers, painful breasts, and difficulties caring for their children. Those who can afford it will seek pain alleviation massages from very few certified physical therapists.

Our novel design integrates a fully automatic mechanical massage system into a comfortable, supportive bra-like garment. Developed in partnership with NYC’s FemFirst Health clinic, MilkShaker replicates massages performed by certified physical therapists—the most effective treatment for mastitis but difficult to access due to limited certifications.

As the only device of its kind, MilkShaker brings professional care into the home, prioritizing function, accessibility, mobility, and safety. With funding from StartupColumbia and others, we envision MilkShaker as standard breastfeeding equipment, HSA-eligible and covered under the Affordable Care Act. Our product gives all mothers a new opportunity to alleviate an inevitable breastfeeding pain.

Automated Professional Massage

The mechanical system performs a continuous, 30-minute professional lymphatic drainage massage based on quantified pressure, force, and direction from certified PTs at FemFirst.

Portable & Hands Free

Our rechargeable technology allows for the mobility of new mothers. With 10 massages per overnight charge, new mothers can perform daily tasks without sacrificing their health.

Washable, Removable Technology

Our mechanical massager sits within the breast pockets of the wearable vest-like garment, allowing for complete electronics removal for easy machine washing and part replacement.

Direct Pushing Massage

Certified lymphatic massages involve a light, pushing motion unlike deep massagers. Our proprietary mechanism is the only option on the market providing this science-backed massage.

Breast-Specific

MilkShaker, both preventative and a treatment device, targets the root cause of mastitis via light, directional massage proven to stimulate lymphatic fluid flow.
